Snowboard Recovery Complete-ish?

Two months after busting my ass and face at Okemo snowboarding, the recovery is finally over (kinda). I went off a jump to 360, which turned into a 720 due to over-rotation. Landed face/shoulder first at the end of the jump. Sad to say it happened near the beginning of my trip to Okemo with my friend Vlad, and sitting back while people ski is not fun. I got real good at puzzles, though!

I had a level 2 concussion, fractured cheek, bloody left desu eye, sprained MCL, torn rotator cuff, and newly developing TMJ! Face is fixed and PT will fix the knee, but my jaw will be clicking for a while and my teeth are still funky. On the bright side, I don't have dental right now which is dope.

So long, sweettooth. Always wear your helmet, kids! Who knows what I woulda ended up like without one. Ski season 2018-2019, sayonara!
